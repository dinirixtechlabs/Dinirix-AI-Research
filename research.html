Scientific Discovery

AI & Computational Research

Conducting fundamental research to establish the next generation of safe, reliable, and energy-efficient neural architectures.

Active Research Areas

Our labs are structured into eight specialized research divisions, each focusing on a core facet of machine intelligence.

Computer Vision

Pioneering multi-spectral image segmentation, real-time object tracking, and deep spatial mapping for dynamic environment interpretation.

Large Language Models

Investigating neuro-symbolic reasoning, token-efficient pre-training, and context extension techniques in generative transformers.

Robotics & Control

Developing reinforcement learning frameworks for physical quadrupeds, dexterous manipulators, and zero-shot sim-to-real transfer.

Healthcare AI

Applying generative diffusion models to de novo protein folding, disease pathway prediction, and rapid diagnostic imaging audits.

Smart Cities

Designing decentralized, multi-agent systems for grid load balancing, traffic congestion forecasting, and autonomous public transport routing.

Autonomous Systems

Building safety-critical control loops, sensor fusion models (Lidar/Radar/Camera), and trajectory planners for autonomous vehicles.

TinyML

Exploring extreme neural network compression, quantization-aware training, and micro-controller deployment of deep models.

Edge AI

Developing distributed, privacy-preserving federated learning systems that train models directly on local hardware nodes.
