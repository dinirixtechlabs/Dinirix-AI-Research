Research Internship Program

Mentorship, compute resources, and publishing freedom.

Our specialized **AI Research Internship** offers graduate and PhD students the opportunity to work directly with senior scientists on open research problems. Interns at Dinirix are treated as full-time researchers, with access to our state-of-the-art GPU clusters, private datasets, and dedicated compute budgets.

We encourage our interns to publish their findings. A significant portion of our internship projects result in co-authored papers submitted to conferences like NeurIPS, ICML, and CVPR.

Internship Details

  • ✓ Duration: 12 to 24 Weeks (Flexible)
  • ✓ Target: PhD, Master's, or Outstanding Bachelor's
  • ✓ Focus: LLM Reasoning, Sim-to-Real Control, TinyML
  • ✓ Benefits: Competitive stipend, housing allowance, compute grants

Our Hiring Process

Online Application

2

Technical Evaluation

A deep-dive call with a senior engineer covering algorithmic code or reviewing one of your past publications.

3

Panel Presentation

4
